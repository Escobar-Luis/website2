Intro

As the title suggest's, this is my 2nd attempt at creating my personal website. Why?

Well, in my first website, I used the free code camp walkthrough on building a website for my portfolio.

The reason being that I wanted to see how a professional who's been coding for years constructs a website so, I can get first hand experience. Lo and Behold, I was extremely overwhelmed! I was following along well through the html portion until we reached the css.

OMG! The amount of styling that guy used was unbelievable; I couldn't keep up. However, from the experience, I was able to retain the good practice of classifying all my divs so they can be styled more easily with css. As well, learned how to organize my html in a human readble fashion!

Anyways, I didn't feel comfortable of leaving that first website since it wasn't a display of my "True" skill but, of an experienced developer. It's similar to the guilty feeling we get (at least I did), of copying someones homework and handing it as your own. So I re-did it and this website is a display of my current skill and I know its bad! Lol
